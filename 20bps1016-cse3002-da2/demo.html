A BRIEF ABOUT US

Our vision is to reach out to young, school-going kids and help them learn in a more fun and enagaging manner. We aim to acheive this via playful learning, which means we incorporate interactive sessions and games into the kids' regular learning process. Playful learning builds better imagination in children. While playing, children tend to remember different aspects of the game which helps them to accelerate memory growth. The playful quality aims to inspire kids to link things using their imagination and creativity and to investigate different realities and points of view. These worlds give kids the latitude they need to invent and implement new possibilities since they are not constrained by reality.
