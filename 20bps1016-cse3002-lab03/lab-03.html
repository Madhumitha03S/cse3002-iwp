A QUICK INTRODUCTION

India is the seventh-largest country in the world and also the second populous. It lies in southern Asia. It is officially known as the Republic of India. Being a part of the world's oldest civilizations, India has a rich legacy that encompasses a wide variety of faiths, traditions, and customs. The treasure of Indian art, architecture, classical dance, music, flora, and fauna, as well as the ingrained secular mindset of its people, are the highlights of Indian heritage. One of the few democracies in the world, it is governed by a parliamentary form of government. India has 28 states and 8 Union territories for the purpose of administration.
